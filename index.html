01 — About

The Scientist
Behind the Work

I'm a PhD candidate at the Geisel School of Medicine at Dartmouth, working in Dr. George O'Toole's lab. My research sits at the intersection of microbial ecology, gut microbiome science, and translational medicine, focused on how gut bacteria can improve outcomes in cystic fibrosis (CF).

My thesis centers on Bacteroides as a potential CF probiotic. I combine experimental evolution, molecular microbiology, culturomics, and bioinformatics to understand how these bacteria adapt in the CF gut.

Before Dartmouth, I trained in India at Symbiosis International University and the University of Mumbai, developing expertise in lactic acid bacteria, probiotic science, and secondary metabolite biosynthesis. In 2025, I attended the Marine Biological Laboratory's Microbial Diversity course at Woods Hole, MA.

Outside the lab, I'm chasing National Parks, Formula 1 races, and the perfect data visualization.

02 — Research

Core Research Areas

↗ Hover each card to see project photos

🦠

CF Gut Microbiome

Culturomics of colonoscopy samples from CF and non-CF individuals — one of the largest gut culture collections of its kind to understand dysbiosis in cystic fibrosis.

🦠
🧬

Bacteroides as CF Probiotic

Evolving Bacteroides strains for enhanced fitness in CF-like environments; decoding molecular mechanisms including bile acid resistance via Diversity-Generating Retroelements.

🧬
🔬

Microbial Ecology

Spatial and genomic ecology of Gracilibacteria in green sulfur bacteria enrichment cultures using metagenomics and high-resolution imaging at MBL Woods Hole (2025).

🔬
⚗️

Probiotic LAB Biology

Isolated and characterized novel L. plantarum strains; evolved tolerance to furfural and 5-HMF for lignocellulosic fermentations. Three first-author publications.

⚗️
🌿

Streptococus ruoffii sp. nov.

Isolated and characterized Streptococus ruoffii sp. nov. The new species was isolated from esophagus of adult male heterozygous for CF allele.

🌿
💻

Bioinformatics

Genome assembly, annotation, comparative genomics, phylogenetics, transcriptomics, and microbiome analysis using R, Python, and Linux. Metagenomics for complex communities.

💻

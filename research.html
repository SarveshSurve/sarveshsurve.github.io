02 — Research

Six threads
of curiosity.

From culturing the unculturable to evolving bacteria in the lab — a tour of the questions I'm currently chasing.

CF Gut Microbiome

Culturomics of colonoscopy samples from CF and non-CF individuals — building one of the largest gut culture collections of its kind to understand dysbiosis in cystic fibrosis.

Bacteroides as CF Probiotic

Evolving Bacteroides strains for enhanced fitness in CF-like environments; decoding molecular mechanisms including bile acid resistance via Diversity-Generating Retroelements.

Microbial Ecology

Spatial and genomic ecology of Gracilibacteria in green sulfur bacteria enrichment cultures using metagenomics and high-resolution imaging at MBL Woods Hole (2025).

Probiotic LAB Biology

Isolated and characterized novel L. plantarum strains; evolved tolerance to furfural and 5-HMF for lignocellulosic fermentations. Three first-author publications.

Streptococcus ruoffii sp. nov.

Isolated and characterized Streptococcus ruoffii sp. nov. — a new species described from the esophagus of an adult male heterozygous for the CF allele.

Bioinformatics

Genome assembly, annotation, comparative genomics, phylogenetics, transcriptomics, and microbiome analysis using R, Python, and Linux. Metagenomics for complex communities.
